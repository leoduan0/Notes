# Structure

## First paragraph

- Outline (define):
  - Concepts
  - Theories

## Second paragraph

- Describe the study
  - Aim
  - Procedure
  - Findings
- Note that no critical thinking is required. It is often discouraged since it obscures the content.

## Third paragraph

- Link the study/concept/question (how does the study demonstrate theory or concept)
